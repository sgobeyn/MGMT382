What to wear on your hang-gliding adventure!

Please arrive to your scheduled visit wearing comfortable clothing: a t-shirt, long pants, jacket or windbreaker, closed-toe shoes, & thick socks. DO NOT wear clothes that restrict your movement. Regardless of the daily temperatures, we suggest a few layers of clothing as wind and high altitudes can create an uncomfortably cold experience.
